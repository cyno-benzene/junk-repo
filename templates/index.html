Phone Number Encoder

Your number is safe no need to worry !

Refresh or regenerate to change color • Right click to save image • Click on title to reset

This just takes the phone number as input, and converts its 256 bit hash array to PNG images that are just represented vibrantly. We have used SHA256 algorithm, the alphanumeric is simply converted to integers. These integers with 64 characters are then reshaped into 8x8 array which is then plotted with colors. And therefore We get the image with an abstract and unique pattern which can be used as a sticker on their phone :)
