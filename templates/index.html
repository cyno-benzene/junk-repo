Phone Number Encoder

Your number is safe no need to worry !

Refresh to change color • Right click to save image • Click on title to reset

The tool just converts phone number to PNG images that be used as a colorful way to share numbers easily. There is no special algorithm used, the number is simply converted to binary. The first 32-33 something bytes in the image can help you calculate the number. Remaining squares are shuffled or reversed strings concantenated according the number of bits (currently just shows 32bits , value can be changed upto 128bit). The idea is to have a way to share phone numbers just like QR codes. Rather the image leaves an abstract pattern which one can stick on to his phone :)
